Why Does English Pronunciation Feel So Unpredictable?

English has one of the most complex spelling systems in the world. The same letter can produce five different sounds. The same sound can be spelled in twelve different ways. Most learners spend years memorising words one at a time — without ever understanding the system underneath.

The result? Progress feels random. You can study a word for months and still hesitate when you need to say it out loud. You second-guess yourself in conversations. You hold back.

SoundCode 44 solves this at the root. By mastering the 44 phonemes of British Received Pronunciation — the complete set of sounds the English language is built from — you gain a framework that makes every word more predictable, every sentence more natural, and every conversation more confident.
